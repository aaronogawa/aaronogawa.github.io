Hi, I'm Aaron!

I'm a photographer based in Los Angeles focusing to capture and create narratives through the lens of my camera. I initially began my photography journey working at USC for the El Rodeo Yearbook and The Daily Trojan, where I photographed sports and campus events. In my junior year I joined elevASIAN, the Asian American vertical at USC Annenberg Media.

As a photojournalist and editor, storytelling is the forefront of my objective. Through my photographs I capture the essence of artists, primarily in the music industry, to reflect their identity and bring along the viewer to the live event as if they were there.
